Terminology

Rz (µm)
Mean peak-to-valley height across a surface trace; used here for headline surface finish.
Ra / RMS
Arithmetic mean (or RMS) deviation of heights; appears in field PDFs but not used for site-wide comparisons.
T30 / T60
Time for energy decay by 30 dB / 60 dB extrapolated; reported by octave band.
Sabine / Eyring
Reverberation time models relating volume, surface area, and absorption; used for sanity bounds.
Schroeder frequency
Transition between modal and diffuse field behavior; below it, discrete modes dominate.
