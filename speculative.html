Speculative Hypotheses

Theory These are defended interpretations to provoke investigation. They are not claims of fact.

1) 34–35 Hz intentionality

Several chambers express a fundamental near 34–35 Hz. Hypothesis: geometry was tuned for a tactile bass mode to scaffold ascetic practice.

  • Repeatability: wavelength-scale dimensions across rooms.
  • Sensitivity: small geometric changes shift the mode.
  • Mechanism: drones and recitation excite the mode; vibration is felt as much as heard.

2) Cosmotechnics of polish

Mirror granite is not mere finish but medium. The polish "stores" abrasion trajectories; optics amplify presence; the cave becomes an instrument.

3) Empire & asceticism as a coupled system

Ashokan patronage to a determinist sect creates architecture that enforces sonic determinism—ritualizing niyati in stone.

Falsification hooks: detune geometry (ΔL) in simulation and measure modal drift; compare to chamber differences and observed peaks.
