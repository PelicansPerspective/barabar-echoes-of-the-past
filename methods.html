Methods & Data

All raw/derived files are available via Zenodo DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.16870852.

Surface metrology

We report surface finish as Rz (µm), the mean peak-to-valley height across traces. Ra/RMS values may appear in field PDFs but are not used for headline comparisons on this site.

  • Traces & summaries: surface_roughness_rz.csv (repo data/)
  • Probe parameters, sampling length/cutoff: see Zenodo deposit

Acoustics

We use swept-sine excitation and impulse-response analysis. Results are reported as T30/T60 by octave band (31.5–8k Hz). Narrow-band resonances are noted separately.

  • Impulse responses (.wav): Zenodo deposit
  • Banded decay (T30/T60): acoustics_bands.csv
  • Peak summaries for figures: acoustics_summary.csv

Note "~70 s" refers to low-frequency peaks (e.g., 50–63 Hz). Mid/high bands are much shorter.

Geometry

Dimensions and volumes derive from laser/structured-light scans. Reported tolerances are typically 1–5 mm; see scan logs.

  • Dimensions: geometry_dimensions.csv
  • Merged overview: barabar_caves_comprehensive_data.csv
