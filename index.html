Surface Precision Analysis

This analysis demonstrates the extraordinary smoothness of Barabar cave walls, with roughness grades significantly superior to modern engineering achievements. The caves achieve an RG of 0.466, making them approximately 20 times smoother than modern granite surfaces.

Acoustic Properties

The acoustic properties of Barabar caves are unprecedented, with reverberation times reaching 72 seconds in Gopika Cave. This far exceeds famous architectural spaces and suggests sophisticated understanding of acoustic engineering principles.
